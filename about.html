About Us

Metro Mirchi is nestled in small but big enough to accomodate and satisfy the desire of every foodie where nature meets the gastronomic desire of foodies. Adorned with dark reclaimed woodwork, exposed spotless white brick walls, antique light fixtures and vintage décor, groovy music the restaurant looks like it could have been there for a century, though its’ doors opened in this new millennium.

Metro Mirchi started on 15th of June 2012 to bring change in the restaurant industry in Patna. Metro Mirchi is totally a contemporary concept bringing foodie and food together and making it more happening place in Patna. As Bihar has Become a Dry state under our chief Minister Mr Nitish Kumar, so very few places where youth and family could go and enjoy their self apart from normal indian restaurant serving before us in Patna.

With Metro Mirchi we have brought a change in the restaurant industry in Bihar where we have Live Music which happens on weekends and we call different artists to perform. Our cuisine is totally fusion to blend with the taste of Patna.
